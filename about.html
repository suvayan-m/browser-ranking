About

I made this project to practice my skills, and whatever I had learned from the past few months. If you are reading this you may use this website to get a rough overview and ranking of web browsers based on privacy.

Somehow I landed on the site https://privacytests.org/ after watching a YouTube video and was amazed by the data they had got. I noticed that they were providing the data in JSON format also so, I thought this would be a great chance to practice what I have learnt.

I used JavaScript to fetch the data and with the help of that data, I calculated the score for each browser. I got 141 test results from the JSON for each browser in the case of the desktop browsers so for every pass I gave +1 then I divided the total by the total number of tests performed and then took the percentage multiplying the result by 100. Therefore the scores are out of 100.

Now as you can some of them got positive and some of them got negative rankings. I have ranked the highest-scoring browser as the best as you can see in the website itself.

You may visit https://privacytests.org/ for more information and read their About page here.
